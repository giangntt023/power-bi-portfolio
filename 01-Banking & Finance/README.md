# 🏦 Aurora Bank - Customer Insights & Fraud Detection Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This end-to-end Power BI business intelligence solution designed for the banking sector. The project focuses on transforming raw transactional and customer data into actionable insights, focusing on  **Customer Segmentation**,  **Credit Risk Analysis**,  **Fraud Detection**, and  **Customer Value Modeling (CLV)** .

The goal is to help bank executives and risk management teams to gain a clear understanding of generational spending trends, evaluate portfolio credit health through Debt-to-Income (DTI), Credit utilization metrics, optimize operations, enhance customer retention, and detect fraudulent activity.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

In this project, I addressed the following key business challenges:

* **Analyze transaction behavior** to identify spending trends and anomalies.
* **Segment customers** using behavioral characteristics and financial health.
* **Evaluate customer value** through CLV metrics across generations.
* **Risk Mitigation:** Detecting anomalous/fraudulent transactions before they impact the bank's bottom line.
* **Deliver actionable insights** for fraud risk management and business growth.
